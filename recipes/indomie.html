Kawach Noodles

Description

A special twist on the usual indomie noodles as cooked by my dear mother.

Ingredients

  • Indomie noodles
  • Sossi soya chunks
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the sossi chunks in a pan
  2. Boil the indomie noodles in a separate pan
  3. Fry the onions and tomatoes in a pan
  4. Add the sossi chunks and indomie noodles
  5. Serve hot with the grated cheese on top
