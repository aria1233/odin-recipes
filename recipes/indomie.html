Kawach Noodles

Description

A special twist on the usual indomie noodles as cooked by my dear mother.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Indomie noodles
  • 1 packet of Sossi soya chunks
  • 2 cups of Water
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 sliced Tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Fry the onions and tomatoes in a pan
  2. Boil the indomie noodles in a separate pan
  3. Add the sossi chunks to the onions and tomato
  4. Add water to the mixture
  5. After the sossi chunks are well cooked add the noodles and mix thoroughly
  6. Serve hot with the grated cheese on top
