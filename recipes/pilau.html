Pilau

Description

A tasty dish cooked using rice, meat and masala spice. It is a popular dish in East Africa especially in Kenya and Tanzania. It is served with Kachumbari(a mixture of raw tomatoes and onions) to enhance its flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 diced Onions
  • 1 Sachet of Tomato Paste
  • 300 gms of Meat
  • 2 cups of Rice
  • 4 cups of Water
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil the meat and water in a pan
  2. Once the meat is cooked strain the broth and set it aside
  3. Fry the onions in a pan until they're golden brown
  4. Add the tomato paste and stir
  5. Add the meat and washed rice into the pan and stir until they're well mixed
  6. Add the bone broth to the rice mixture and allow it to cook
  7. Serve with kachumbari
