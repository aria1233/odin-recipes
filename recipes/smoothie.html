Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

Breakfast, lunch, dessert, or snack time, any time's the right time for this delicious banana smoothie.

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Milk
  • Peanut butter
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Slice bananas into chunks
  2. Place bananas, milk,peaut butter and honey into a blender
  3. Serve chilled with ice cubes
